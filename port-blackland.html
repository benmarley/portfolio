Blackland Sell Sheet

The creators of the short film, Blackland requested that I create a sell sheet for thier pitch to Cineplex (the potential producers of the film). For this job I was asked to incorperate torn notes, a map, and other imagery that I thought would go well for the narrative. I created all assets in computer from the map, to the note. I took all photographs myself as well.
